Our Mission

Technology that serves
the people around you

Our mission is to help students build core technical skills while supporting small businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations that need stronger digital visibility.

Through a structured curriculum, students learn the fundamentals of web and app creation — including design, development, user interface customization, and project planning.

We believe technology should be useful, accessible, and service-driven. By combining STEM education, entrepreneurship, and community service, Local Launch gives students early exposure to real-world development while helping local businesses grow their digital presence.

What We Do

Beyond the classroom

01

Learn the Fundamentals

Students master web and app development through hands-on lessons in design, coding, UI customization, and project planning.

02

Identify Local Needs

Students connect with local businesses and organizations, understand their challenges, and define project goals together.

03

Build Real Solutions

Websites, apps, and digital tools are built to solve real problems — not hypothetical exercises.

04

Measure Impact

Students present their work, reflect on outcomes, and see how their skills directly help their community grow.

Local Launch Competition

Compete, collaborate,
and make an impact

Through our Local Launch Competition, students work individually or in small teams to set project goals, develop solutions, present their work, and measure their impact.

  • Set ambitious project goals with real stakeholders
  • Develop websites, apps, or digital tools
  • Present solutions to peers and community members
  • Track and measure community impact
